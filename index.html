Have you heard of Wwoof?

Wwoof stands for World Wide Opportunities on Organic Farms its a global network. Volunteers (WWOOFers) stay and help on the land and hosts provide the food and accommodation. When I was university student I wwoof in Australia for one and a half months.It was a life changing experience learning about organic agriculture, eating delicious food, made a lot of foreign friends. I'll share you my experiences!
